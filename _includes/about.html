I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Chair of Symmetric Cryptography at Ruhr University Bochum (RUB). Before joining RUB, I held a postdoctoral position at CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security from January 2021 to December 2023. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Indian Statistical Institute (ISI) Kolkata in June 2020 under the supervision of Prof. Mridul Nandi.
My research interests are primarily in the theory and practice of cryptology, with a focus on practice-oriented provable security of symmetric-key cryptography. If you are interested in this research direction, you may also enjoy Online GAPS, a recurring online seminar that I co-organise.
